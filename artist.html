Formed of well kneaded native earth
Like everyone else
That forms thousands of great men
Thousands of pretty women
Millenia's civilised grace
So well kneaded and formed
Great Gautama Buddha. fair Mira Bai
and Jamil Naqsh,
Formed of well kneaded earth
To return to the same old earth
Others of the kind to be born to confirm my being.

Jamil Naqsh 1997






Born in Kairana UP, India, in 1939. The town is renowned the world over for its musicians of the classical Kairana. Jamil Naqsh's father, a zamindar, wrote books on topics including tib and nafsiyat and illustrated his works in miniature style.

Artists, writers, poets, musicians, zamindars and politicians often gathered in his ancestral home and poetry, music, painting were a way of life.

This stimulating environment left an indelible mark on the mind of the young Jamil Naqsh, and encouraged him to paint. Got early art education 1953-1954 from Mayo school of arts Lahore, now NCA under the tutelage of miniature painter Ustad Shariff.

Jamil Naqsh is both a modern expressionist and a traditional miniaturist.
Artist presently work & lives in London
